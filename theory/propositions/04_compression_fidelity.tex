\subsubsection{3.3 proofframework (Proof
Sketch)}\label{ux8bc1ux660eux6846ux67b6-proof-sketch}

\textbf{Step 1 --- sensitivity分解}

will误差重写provides加权and形式.Let
\(a_i = 1 - w_i \cdot \mathbf{1}\{i \in C_s\}\),then

\[
R_S(f) - R_C(f) = \frac{1}{N_s} \sum_{i \in S_s} a_i \cdot \ell_f(x_i, y_i),
\]

where \(\sum_i a_i = 0\)(guaranteed byweights归一化保证).guaranteed byAssume (CA3),\(\{a_i\}\)
satisfy:

\[
|a_i| \leq \max\!\left\{
\underbrace{\frac{N_s}{n_s'} \cdot \frac{e^{\tau}}{\bar{w}} - 1}_{\text{保留sampleUpper bound}},
\; \underbrace{1}_{\text{not yet保留sampleUpper bound}}
\right\}
\leq \frac{N_s}{n_s'} \cdot \frac{e^{\tau}}{\bar{w}},
\]

where \(\bar{w} = \frac{1}{n_s'} \sum_{i \in C_s} e^{-\tau D_i}\)
providesaveraging指数weights,\(\tau\) provides代码 \texttt{temperature} parameter.

\textbf{Step 2 --- we have效sample量and冗余relationship}

definitionwe have效sample量 (effective sample size):

\[
N_{\text{eff}} = \frac{(\sum w_i)^2}{\sum w_i^2} = \frac{N_s^2}{\sum_{i \in C_s} w_i^2}.
\]

as \(D_{\text{eff}}(s)\)
较High,\(w_i \approx N_s/n_s'\)(eachsampleweights接近),\(N_{\text{eff}} \approx n_s'\);as
\(D_{\text{eff}}(s)\)
较Low,weightsdiverges,\(N_{\text{eff}} < n_s'\).具体地:

\[
N_{\text{eff}} \;\geq\; n_s' \cdot \bigl(1 - D_{\text{eff}}(s)\bigr)^{-1}.
\tag{8}
\]

the式建立了 \(D(s)\) and采样Efficiency之间桥梁:High冗余意味着Highwe have效sample量.

\textbf{Step 3 --- shoulduses uniform convergence 界}

For fixed加权Mechanism,auditingLoss复合类 \(\mathcal{L}_{\mathcal{F}}\) shoulduses标准
uniform convergence bound(see Bartlett \& Mendelson, 2002; Bousquet et
al., 2004):

以probabilistic \(\geq 1 - \delta\),

\[
\sup_{f \in \mathcal{F}} \bigl| R_S(f) - R_C(f) \bigr|
\;\leq\; 2 \cdot \mathfrak{R}_{N_{\text{eff}}}(\mathcal{L}_{\mathcal{F}})
\;+\; B \cdot \sqrt{\frac{\log(2/\delta)}{2 N_{\text{eff}}}}
\;+\; \mathbb{E}\bigl[|R_S - R_C|\bigr],
\]

where \(\mathfrak{R}_{m}(\mathcal{L}_{\mathcal{F}})\) is \(m\) sampleson
Rademacher Complexity.guaranteed byAssume (CA2)

\textbf{Step 4 --- Substituting \(N_{\text{eff}}\) and \(n_s'\) relationship}

will式 (8) Substituting,并结合式 (3) \(n_s'\) 显式形式:

\[
\frac{1}{\sqrt{N_{\text{eff}}}}
\;\leq\; \sqrt{\frac{1 - D_{\text{eff}}(s)}{n_s'}}
\;\leq\; \sqrt{\frac{1 - D_{\text{eff}}(s)}{\alpha \sqrt{N_s} + \beta B_s N_s + \gamma \bar{r}(s)\sqrt{N_s}}}.
\]

\[

\]

mergei.e.,we get式 (7).\(\square\)
