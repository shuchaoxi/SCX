\section{Proposition 3: State-Conditioned Expert
Weighting}\label{proposition-3-state-conditioned-expert-weighting}

\begin{quote}
Optimal expert weights are not global constants, but state-conditioned functions.The expected risk of state-conditioned weights is no higher than any globally fixed weight.
\end{quote}
